# SOP: Good Distribution Practice (GDP)

:::info Regulatory Reference
EU GDP Guidelines (2013/C 343/01) & WHO GSDP
:::

## 1. Objective
To ensure medicinal products are stored, transported, and handled under suitable conditions.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all warehouse receiving, storage, and shipping operations.

## 3. Procedure
### 3.1 Receipt of Goods
* Incoming stock must be verified against Purchase Orders.
* Check for physical damage and tamper seals.
