# SOP: Change Control Management

## 1. Objective
To manage changes to validated systems, equipment, and processes to prevent unintended impact on product quality.

## 2. Process Flow
1. **Initiation:** Raise Change Request (CR).
2. **Impact Assessment:** QA and SME review.
3. **Approval:** Pre-approval required before execution.
4. **Execution & Verification:** Implement change and test.
5. **Closure:** Final QA sign-off.
